Sarcosuchus imperator

The Sarcosuchus is a large sized, crocodyliform carnivore found in Path of Titans. Sarcosuchus is a semi-aquatic creature, and takes on a more ambush focused playstyle compared to other semi-aquatics in the game.

Sarcosuchus is the only crocodyliform in Path of Titans, setting it aside from every other creature in the game. It has a long and narrow snout full of teeth, with large rectangular scutes along its back. Sarcosuchus has a long, powerful tail that allows it to swim quickly after prey. Sarcosuchus is low to the ground and usually slower than most other creatures in Path of Titans, but has a surprisingly quick sprint for ambushing players.


Living period: Cretaceous period 145-66 millions years ago


Abilities:

Bite

Causes medium damage.

Ripping Bite

An attack that causes heavy bleed damage.

Scavenger Metabolism

Can eat fish, meat, carrion, shellfish, and bones. Fast food and water drain.

Paddle

Increases swim speed, swim turning radius, and oxygen. drain.
